Let's fight gender inequality in STEM

Our project focuses on creating a better support system for women and girls in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) fields. We chose this topic because we have experienced a lack of support and representation in our STEM classes throughout high school. We are both looking to continue on a science/math trajectory in college and potential future career, and so these issues are very important to us. With this project, we have created a place where women and girls can connect and help each other feel supported in STEM.
